Difficulty levels
Easy
One concept, 3–8 lines. Core SQL only — SELECT, WHERE, JOIN, GROUP BY, CASE. No CTEs or window functions.
Medium
Two or more concepts combined, 8–18 lines. Multi-table JOINs, conditional aggregation, subqueries, or a simple CTE. Equivalent to most interview problems.
Hard
CTEs and/or window functions required, 15–30 lines. DENSE_RANK, LAG/LEAD, running totals, recursive CTEs. Mid-to-senior level interview territory.
